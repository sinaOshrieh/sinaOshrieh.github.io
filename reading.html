Below, you can find some of my favourite books! I've recently read these and have learned a lot from each of them

Renuka Gavrani explores the common of them loneliness and the implication that people who are alone are unworthy of love, are faulty, and have issues they need to resolve before they can find friends and feel truly accepted. All of these elements are debunked in this book which encourages people to emrbace their peace and space.

In this book, Don Miguel Ruiz doffer a powerful code of conduct that can rapidly transform our lives to a new experience of freedom, true happiness, and love based on ancient Toltec wisdom!

This book by Brianna Wiest delves deep into ways of thinking that can teach you something new and make your life better. The book will introduce you to ways of thinking in your daily routine that can change your life forever!

This book by Ichiro Kishimi is really interesting. Using the theories of Alfred Adler, this book follows a dialogue between a philosopher and a young man. Over the course of five conversations, the philosopher helps his student understand how each of us is able to determine the direction of our own life and the judgments of others.
